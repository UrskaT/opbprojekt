RENAULT Grand Scenic 2016 - Present

Home > Cars > RENAULT > Grand Scenic > Grand Scenic 2016 - Present
The 2016 Renault Grand Scenic was introduced shortly after the launch of the new generation Scenic compact MPV. Styled in the same manner, the new Grand Scenic features a two-tone color setup, chrome or metal grey accessories, smooth flowing surfaces on the sides, 20-inch rims as standard, 3D-effect taillamps, rear bumper with integrated diffuser, fixed panoramic roof and matte aluminum roof bars. The modular interior can take up to seven passengers and features a nice, clean design, with an uncluttered dashboard and a "floating" center console that holds a vertically-mounted 8.7-inch display running the R-LINK 2 infotainment system. Other features include lane keeping assist, driver fatigue detection, adaptive cruise control, active emergency braking, lane departing warning, traffic sign recognition, blind spot monitoring, color head-up display and more.
